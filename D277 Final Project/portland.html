Portland Population

The estimated population of Portland, OR as of 2021 is around 641,162, according to the United States Census Bureau.

All of the information on this page was cited from:

U.S. Census Bureau
Portland Year Incorporated

Portland, OR was incorporated as a city in 1851.


Portland Region

Portland, OR is located in the northwest region of the state, near the confluence of the Willamette and Columbia Rivers.


Portland City Type

Portland, OR is classified as an urban city.


Portland Income Level

According to the U.S. Census Bureau, the median household income in Portland, OR is $75,292, which is slightly above the median household income for the state of Oregon, which is $72,502.
