Bend Population

The estimated population of Bend, OR as of 2021 is around 102,059, according to the United States Census Bureau.

All of the information on this page was cited from:

U.S. Census Bureau
Bend Year Incorporated

Bend, OR was incorporated as a city in 1905.


Bend Region

Bend, OR is located in the central region of the state, in the high desert of the eastern Cascade Range.


Bend City Type

Bend, OR is classified as a suburban city.


Bend Income Level

According to the U.S. Census Bureau, the median household income in Bend, OR is $78,436, which is slightly higher than the median household income for the state of Oregon, which is $72,502.
