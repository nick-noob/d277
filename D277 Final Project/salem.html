Salem Population

The estimated population of Salem, OR as of 2021 is around 177,723, according to the United States Census Bureau.

All of the information on this page was cited from:

U.S. Census Bureau
Salem Year Incorporated

Salem, OR was incorporated as a city in 1857.


Salem Region

Salem, OR is located in the Willamette Valley region of the state.


Salem City Type

Salem, OR is classified as an urban city.


Salem Income Level

According to the U.S. Census Bureau, the median household income in Salem, OR is $65,157, which is slightly below the median household income for the state of Oregon, which is $72,502.
