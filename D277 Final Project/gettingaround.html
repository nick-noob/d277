TriMet MAX Light Rail

This is a light rail system that runs throughout the Portland metropolitan area and provides easy access to downtown Portland, as well as to other neighborhoods and suburbs.

TriMet MAX
TriMet Bus

TriMet operates a comprehensive bus network throughout the Portland metropolitan area, with over 80 different bus routes that provide connections to various destinations.

TriMet Bus
Portland Streetcar

This is a modern streetcar system that serves several neighborhoods in downtown Portland, including the Pearl District, South Waterfront, and Northwest Portland.

Portland Streetcar
Portland Aerial Tram

The Portland Aerial Tram is a unique transportation option that connects the South Waterfront neighborhood to the main campus of Oregon Health & Science University (OHSU) on Marquam Hill.

Portland Aerial Tram
Biketown

Biketown is a bike-sharing program that allows riders to rent bicycles for short-term use. The program has over 1,500 bikes available at over 180 different stations throughout the city.

Biketown
Commuter Rail

Portland also has a commuter rail system operated by TriMet called the Westside Express Service (WES), which provides service between Beaverton and Wilsonville.
