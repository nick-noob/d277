Our Favorite Irish Pub: Kells

Portland is home to the greatest Irish Pub in all the land. Kells Brew Pub is owned and operated by Garrett "The Legend" McAleese.

Some of the best dishes include:

  • Kells Hazy IPA
  • Kells Fries
  • Pub Pretzels
  • Sausage Roll
  • Shepherd's Pie
  • Dublin Chicken Curry
